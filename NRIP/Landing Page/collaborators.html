Despite global technological advances in generating risk information on climate-related hazards, vulnerability, and exposure, many countries face challenges in making risk information accessible and usable to inform policies, plans and programming in development and humanitarian settings. Drawing on multiple sources, NRIP aggregates risk information to support national planning processes and investment to achieve the objectives of the Sendai Framework on Disaster Risk Reduction, the Paris Agreement, and the SDGs.
