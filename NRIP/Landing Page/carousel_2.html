NRIP: Coverage and Data

NRIP is designed for scaleable coverage across multiple countries, with the first cluster including Costa Rica, Somalia, South Sudan, Sudan, Eswatini, the Maldives, Bangladesh, and Fiji. Additional countries will be added to NRIP, with a target of 52 countries by the end of 2022, and reaching 100 countries by the end of 2023. NRIP is a key tool under the Global Risk Assessment Framework.

To support better access to and sharing of risk information, NRIP draws from multiple sources to provide the best available risk, hazard, exposure, vulnerability, loss and damage, and climate data. NRIP draws risk information that is high quality, reliable, and authoritative from government and UN sources, as well as global, regional, and third-party organizations.
